Description

Discover tranquility at this charming hillside chalet in Al-Salt. Surrounded by stunning mountain views, this peaceful retreat offers a perfect blend of rustic charm and modern comfort. The private garden provides a serene space to unwind, while the cozy interior invites relaxation after a day of exploration.

Whether you're seeking a quiet escape or a family getaway, this chalet offers everything you need for an unforgettable stay. Experience the beauty of Al-Salt's countryside and enjoy the fresh air and scenic views that make this location unique.
