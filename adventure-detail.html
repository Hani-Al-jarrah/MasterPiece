Petra Guided Exploration

Petra, Jordan

Discover the beauty of Petra, the ancient rose-red city carved into rock, with a full-day guided tour.

  • Duration 1 day
  • Activity level Easy
  • Includes Guided Tour, Tickets, Lunch, Transportation
From 100 J.D per person
See dates

What you'll do

"Experience the breathtaking beauty of Petra, the world-famous UNESCO World Heritage Site carved into rose-red cliffs."

Your journey begins with a guided exploration of the Siq, a stunning narrow gorge that leads to the iconic Treasury (Al-Khazneh). Marvel at the intricate architecture, learn about Petra’s rich history as a trade hub, and uncover fascinating stories from your knowledgeable guide.

Continue your adventure by exploring the Royal Tombs, the Roman Theater, and the Monastery (Ad-Deir) for stunning panoramic views. Enjoy a traditional Jordanian lunch at a local restaurant and discover the ancient Nabatean culture through this unforgettable experience.

Whether you're a history enthusiast or simply in search of awe-inspiring landscapes, Petra offers something for everyone.

Your Itinerary

First Two Hours - Arrival and Introduction

Your adventure begins at the entrance of Petra, where you'll meet your expert guide. After a brief introduction to the history and significance of Petra, you'll start your journey through the Siq, the narrow canyon that leads to the iconic Treasury.

As you walk through the Siq, your guide will share fascinating stories about the Nabateans, the ancient civilization that built Petra. You'll also learn about the geological formations that make this site so unique.

Read More

Midday - Exploring the Treasury and Main Sites

Arrive at the Treasury (Al-Khazneh), Petra's most famous landmark. Spend time marveling at its intricate carvings and learning about its history. Continue to explore the Street of Facades, the Royal Tombs, and the Roman Theater.

Your guide will provide detailed insights into each site, explaining their architectural significance and the role they played in the Nabatean civilization. Take plenty of photos and soak in the breathtaking views.

Read More

Afternoon - Lunch and Optional Hike to the Monastery

Enjoy a traditional Jordanian lunch at a local restaurant within Petra. After lunch, you can choose to hike up to the Monastery (Ad-Deir), one of Petra's largest monuments, or explore more of the lower trails at your own pace.

The hike to the Monastery is challenging but rewarding, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape. If you prefer a more relaxed experience, your guide can recommend other points of interest to explore.
